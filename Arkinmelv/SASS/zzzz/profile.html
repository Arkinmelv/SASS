About Me

Hi there! My name is Arkin Melvian Dwiansyah and I am an experienced IT Help Desk professional with a background in Telecommunications Engineering. I am currently working at PT Infomedia Solusi Humanika, where I am responsible for monitoring internet networks, coordinating with Telkom branches, and resolving problems related to corporate internet networks.

In addition to my IT background, I am also a skilled Frontend Web Developer and UI/UX with experience working at ConecWorld, where I was responsible for slicing UI/UX designs into code, creating design systems and style guides, and coordinating with tech divisions and backend developers to deliver successful projects.

Prior to my work as a Help Desk professional and Frontend Developer, I was a Telecommunications Technician at PT. Wahana Era Sejahtera, where I was responsible for splicing fiber optic cables, managing fiber optic installations, and coordinating with Telkom technicians and supervisors.

I received my Associate's degree in Telecommunications Engineering from Politeknik Negeri Malang, where I also earned a certification in Teknik Instalasi Fiber Optic Madya from Badan Nasional Sertifikasi Profesi.

Outside of my professional work, I was an active member of the Himpunan Mahasiswa Elektro Politeknik Negeri Malang during my time as a student.

I am dedicated to providing exceptional service, solving problems, and staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends in my field. Thank you for taking the time to learn a little bit more about me!
